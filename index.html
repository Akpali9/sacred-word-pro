          Ancient Scriptures
          Apocrypha & 1 Enoch — Historical Witness

          The Apocrypha and the Book of Enoch represent ancient Jewish texts that shaped early Christian thought. Enoch is quoted in the Epistle of Jude (verses 14-15) and was immensely influential among Second Temple Jews and Church Fathers.

          1 Enoch survives fully only in Ge'ez (Ethiopic). It consists of the Book of Watchers, Parables, Astronomical Book, Dream Visions, and Epistle of Enoch. It describes the fall of the Watchers (angels), cosmic prophecy, and messianic judgment.
